The best movies according to Aaditya

My Top Three movies of all time

The Da Vinci Code

This is the best movie so far as it revolves around mystery, murder and Christ

Interstellar

The cinematography is best and at its peak. It shows how a father takes up his job
to save the world as well as his children. The movie has lot of mind bending science laws.

Angels And Demons

The movie follows a murder trail which needs to be solved before midnight , else
The Vatican City will be vanished from the face of the earth. A symbologist teams
up with a scientist to solve the crime.
